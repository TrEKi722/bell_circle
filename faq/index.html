Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Bell Circle? +

Bell Circle is a simple, fast way to view school bell schedules and time-based information without digging through PDFs, announcements, or outdated web pages. It's built to be clear, reliable, and easy to use on any device.

Who is Bell Circle for? +

Bell Circle is designed for students, teachers, and staff who want to quickly know what schedule is running today and what time each period starts and ends. It's especially useful on special schedules like minimum days, assemblies, or testing days.

Is Bell Circle affiliated with my school? +

Bell Circle is an independent project and is not officially affiliated with any school unless explicitly stated. It exists to make schedule information easier to access.

Is Bell Circle free to use? +

Yes. Bell Circle is free to access and does not require payment to view schedules.

Account & Access

Do I need an account to use Bell Circle? +

No account is required for use. You can open the site and immediately see the current schedule and bell times.

Does Bell Circle work on phones? +

Yes. Bell Circle is fully responsive and works well on phones, tablets, and desktops. It's designed to be readable at a glance, even between classes. If you have any issues with the mobile website feel free to contact us, we are always trying to improve the mobile experience.

Does Bell Circle collect personal data? +

Bell Circle does not require personal information. Any data collection is minimal and focused on improving site performance and reliability, and does not contain any PII (Personally Identifiable Information).

Schedules & Updates

How accurate are the schedules? +

Schedules are sourced directly from the school's published information and updated as changes occur. While Bell Circle aims to be highly accurate, it's always smart to double-check official announcements for last-minute changes. If you find something is wrong, feel free to let us know.

How often is the site updated? +

Updates are made whenever new schedules are released or changes are announced. Special schedules are typically added as soon as they're confirmed. Please keep in mind that this site is managed by a team of ONE so data is not always accurate.

What should I do if a schedule looks wrong? +

If you notice an error or outdated information, report it using the contact page or via email at bellcircle@ekinney.com. Corrections are prioritized to keep the information accurate.

Can my school be added to Bell Circle? +

Support for additional schools depends on demand and available data. If your school isn't supported yet, you can request it through the site's contact page.

Features & Support

Can Bell Circle send notifications? +

Nope. We have not recieved enough interest to make this happened, but if you would like to see it come to feel free to let us know.

How can I contact the team behind Bell Circle? +

You can reach out using the contact information provided on bell-circle.com for questions, feedback, or bug reports. You can also visit our feedback page to submit your input.
